How to Increase Inventory Quantity

Some of our new users are looking for the quantity text box in the product definition to enter the inventory count. With Imonggo, you don't change the quantity directly; instead, you record a transaction that will either increase or decrease the quantity. This way, every item movement is accounted for and is traceable.

This short video will demonstrate to you how to increase your inventory quantity by creating a Receive from Supplier transaction.
