PARTNERS
Imonggo makes it easy for anyone to meet by any business need outside the scope of a POS.

We are looking for partners!
  • Developers who are interested to extend the functionalities of Imonggo through its API
  • System integrators who are interested to include Imonggo as part of their turnkey solutions
  • Software or application companies that are interested to integrate with Imonggo
  • Hardware and POS peripheral vendors who are looking for value-added technology

Imonggo is developed to make business easy to retailers worldwide.

Through the use of the latest technology, we are able to produce the most user-friendly POS system at the lowest cost.

Our partner program seeks to extend this goal. We are looking for allied software and hardware providers that can provide unmatched services to our consumers. Through these partnerships, we aim to help our users reach their business milestones quickly and efficiently, but without sacrificing ease of use.

Interested parties may contact us through our support desk. We look forward to work with you.
