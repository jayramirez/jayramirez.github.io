ABOUT US

Imonggo is the brainchild of Movmento, a technology company driven by its vision to change the retail world. Movmento developed Imonggo to help small-to-medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across the globe run their business. Imonggo is the easiest and most cost-effective point of sale to date, empowering even the smallest of retailers. worldwide.

Movmento Pte. Ltd. is an international technology company that delivers software as a service (SaaS) applications. Movmento is headquartered in Singapore, with sales and marketing based in the United States, and research and development based in the Philippines.

In 2009, Movmento Pte. Ltd. released Imonggo, the first cloud-based point of sale software in the global market. Imonggo's data center is located in Missouri, USA, with backup in Dallas, Texas.
